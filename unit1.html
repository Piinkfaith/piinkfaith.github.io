Purple

Purple is percieved as the royal color. This is because of its history. In a Phoenician city of Tyre, "Tyrain Purple" came from a rare species of sea snails. Because of this, the purple dye was expesensive to get to make clothes. Resulting in only, the really rich being the only people with enough money to buy huge amount of purple material.

Meaning of Purple

Purple has many different types of meanings by association. Here is a list of them:
